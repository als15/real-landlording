# Service Categories & Sub-Categories

This document lists all service categories and their sub-categories supported by the Real Landlording platform.

**Total: 5 groups, 35 categories**

---

## Fix It / Build It (Trades & Technical)

### Roofer
- **Service Needed:** Leak Repair, Full Replacement, Maintenance/Coating, Gutter Repair, Skylight Issue, Other
- **Roof Type:** Flat Roof (Rubber/Bitumen), Shingle (Asphalt), Slate, Fiberglass, Metal, Other

### GC (General Contractor)
- **Service Needed:** Full Gut, Large Rehab, Prep for Listing, Kitchen, Bath, Finish Basement, Addition, Scope for Purchase Decision, Rental Improvement Fund Help, 203k Project

### Plumber/Sewer
- **Service Needed:** Leak, Clog/Blockage, No Hot Water, Low Water Pressure, New Installation, Frozen Pipes, Sewer/Water Line, Sewer Scope, Hydrojetting, Other
- **Fixture Involved:** All, Kitchen Sink, Bathroom Sink, Toilet, Shower/Tub, Water Heater, Main Line, Outdoor Spigot, Basement, Other

### Waterproofing/Moisture
- **Service Needed:** French Drain, Sump Pump, Unknown, Other

### Electrician
- **Service Needed:** Power Outage, Flickering Lights, Dead Outlet, New Wiring, Fixture Install, Panel Install/Upgrade, EV Charger, Other
- **Location:** Interior, Exterior, Other

### HVAC Specialist
- **Equipment Type:** Gas Furnace, Electric Furnace, Boiler, Central AC, Heat Pump, Ductless Mini-Split, Oil Furnace
- **Service Needed:** Routine Maintenance, No Heat, Not Cooling, Strange Noise, Leak, Thermostat Issue, Other

### Exterior
- **Service Needed:** Siding, Windows, Doors, Masonry/Brickwork, Stucco, Decking, Paving, Welding, Fencing

### Flooring
- **Service Needed:** Hardwood Refinishing/Repair, Carpet Installation, LVP/Tile Installation, Other

### Appliance Repair
- **Appliance Type:** Washer/Dryer, Oven/Range, Refrigerator, Dishwasher, Other

### Handyman
- **Service Needed:** General Repair, Assembly, Drywall Patching, Small Painting, Minor Plumbing, Other

### Painting
- **Service Needed:** Unit Turn Painting, Touch-Up, Exterior

### Structural
- **Service Needed:** Foundation, Basement, Joists, Stairs, Other

### Architect/Design
- **Service Needed:** Plans, Zoning, Layout, Design Services, Consulting, Other
